Is this solar installation on a residential or commercial property?
⚠  Residential solar building permits have a flat $125 permit fee (if required). Electrical and mechanical permits are issued separately and may require additional fees.

Commercial solar permit fees are based on the total project valuation, excluding electrical and mechanical work covered under separate permits.
Step 2 of 3
What type of cell tower work?
New cell towers or structural modifications to existing towers are based on total project cost, all labor and materials.
